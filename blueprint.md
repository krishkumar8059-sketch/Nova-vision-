# **App Name**: Nova Vision Studio

## Core Features:

- Cinematic Portfolio Showreel: An immersive video gallery showcasing studio works with custom parallax video players and seamless transitions.
- AI Promo Strategist Tool: An AI-powered tool that allows users to input their campaign goals and receive an intelligently generated storyboard and video script suggestion.
- Service Booking Matrix: Dynamic pricing tiers and scheduling calendar displayed through frosted glassmorphism cards.
- Social Media Growth Dashboard: A visual tool displaying management packages and estimated delivery timelines for social content.
- Interactive Client Portal: Secure area for clients to view project statuses and contact the studio directly via the integrated helpline.
- Kinetic Brand Showcase: Animated headlines using glitch and typewriter effects to narrate the 'Defying Digital Boundaries' philosophy.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary: Metallic Gold (#D4AF37) for a premium and high-end studio aesthetic. Secondary: Deep Obsidian Navy (#05070A) for a mysterious, textured dark background as seen in the brand identity.
- Accent: Amber Glow (#FFBF00) for interactive elements and subtle flowing gradients to guide the viewer's eye.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' for its tech-forward, futuristic feel. Body text: 'Inter' for neutral clarity across informational blocks.
- Minimalist gold outline icons with thin strokes to maintain the brand's sleek and professional visual language.
- Layered depth through Frosted Glassmorphism containers with backdrop-blur, providing a translucent contrast against the dark background.
- High interactivity with smooth parallax scrolling, magnetic hover effects on buttons, and kinetic text-glitch or typewriter animations.
